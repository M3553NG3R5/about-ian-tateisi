About Me - Ian Tateisi

Hello, my name is Ian Tateisi, and I am a passionate Origami Artist. Here's a picture of me along with a few of the things I've made recently

I've been super passionate about origami since I was younger and it's always been a way for me to relax and center myself, especially during tumultuous times within my life. Over the years, I've honed my skills and explored various styles and techniques, from traditional origami to intricate shaping using a technique called wet-folding, modular designs and even some of my own creations.

Origami, for me, is more than just folding paper; it's a form of meditation, a way to express creativity, and a means of storytelling. Each piece I create holds a special meaning or represents a particular moment or emotion.

If you're curious about my work or have questions about origami, feel free to reach out. I'm always happy to connect with fellow enthusiasts or those just beginning their journey in this wonderful art form.
